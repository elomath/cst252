Lab 4: Pseudocoding

Subject

Get in the habit of thinking in terms of pseudocoding before jumping into coding.

Challenges

The only issue we had was getting into the mindset of pseudocoding and devising what we wanted to do for our example tasks

Results

These are the resulting lists of pseudocode we created to match the prompt of this project.

Pseudocode Everday Task

= Making Boiled Eggs

  • 1.you get a deep sauce pan
  • 2. get eggs and put them in the sauce pan
  • 3. you then fill it with water until the eggs are submerged
  • 4. turn on the stove to a high medium heat
  • 5. let boil for 10-12 minutes
  • 6. turn off stove and run eggs under cold water
  • 7. peel under cold water
  • 8. they are ready to eat
